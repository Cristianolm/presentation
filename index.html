About me

Who am I?

How I got here

Academically I have a graduation in Geography and Regional Planning and master in Territory Management with specialization in Remote Sensing and Geographic Information Systems, and some non-curricular internship's related to my academic background. That said, I realized that I had a lack of professional skills related with programming and working with databases. And this is what led me to start learning programming and other topics in the IT field.

My learning of programming starts as soon I finished my Master's thesis; master that ended on the beginning of the covid 19 pandemic lockdown. I from there to here I never stopped learning about the IT field.
